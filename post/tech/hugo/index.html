HuGo!

Jun 1, 2015 #Go #Blog #Pelican

Moved my blog from pelican to Hugo. Though there was nothing wrong with pelican as such, mostly I found a problem with my blog/theme not as update with pelican upstream. Hugo is a static site generator written by Steve Francia (@spf13). It is written in Go & amazingly fast to generate a site. Developing sites with hugo is kind of fun; running hugo in watch mode, you get to see the site as you write. Also hugo has a nice set of themes and rather simplistic approach to organizing content. Hopefully the new avatar of the blog should keep me interested in blogging for a while!
